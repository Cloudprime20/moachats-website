# MoChat: The Ultimate After Effects Collaboration & Design Ecosystem

Unlock a suite of professional tools designed to transform your After Effects workflow from a solo creation process into a high-speed, collaborative experience.

---

## 🚀 Collaboration & Video Review

### **MoPack: Professional Video Review**
*Screenshot: `Mopack.png`*
MoPack is the dedicated video review engine within MoChat. It transforms After Effects into a collaborative workspace. Generate frame-accurate "Momeets" (Review Assets) and share them instantly with teammates or clients.
- **Frame-Accurate Seek**: Sync playback perfectly with your AE playhead.
- **Vector Annotations**: Draw directly on frames with arrows, circles, and freehand tools.
- **Threaded Feedback**: Frame-locked comments that sync in real-time between MoChat and MoPack.
- **Marker Export**: Convert client feedback directly into After Effects markers.

### **Secure E2EE Messaging**
*Screenshot: `End_to_end_encryption.png`, `Group_Chat.png`*
Collaborate with total peace of mind. MoChat features industry-standard end-to-end encryption for all messages and media.
- **Privacy First**: Only you and your recipient can read your project discussions.
- **Group Collaboration**: Create secure rooms for specialized project teams.
- **Smart Attach**: Effortlessly share project files, presets, and renders within the chat.

## 🤖 Creative AI & Asset Management

### **MoPrompt: The Director's AI Tool**
*Screenshot: `MoPrompt.png`*
The most sophisticated AI prompt builder for motion designers. Visualize your shot in 3D before generating with Midjourney or DALL-E.
- **3D Scene Preview**: Adjust camera angles and subjects in a real-time 3D viewport.
- **Cinematic Controls**: Professional lens presets (14mm to 200mm), f-stop (bokeh), and lighting styles (Rembrandt, Golden Hour, Neon).
- **Video Motion Paths**: Set Keyframe A and B for complex camera moves like Dolly Zooms and 360° Orbits.
- **Director's Guide**: Built-in education system for cinematic framing and lighting.

### **Google Fonts & Icons Integration**
*Screenshot: `Google_Font.png`, `Google_Icons.png`*
Access the entire Google Fonts and Material Icons library directly inside After Effects.
- **Instant Preview**: See how fonts look on your text layers before applying.
- **One-Click Apply**: Sync fonts and icons to your project without leaving the plugin.

### **Pixabay Stock Integration**
*Screenshot: `Pixabay.png`*
Browse millions of free stock photos, videos, and music tracks. Drag and drop high-quality assets directly into your composition.

## 🎨 Design & Layout Suite

### **Gradlette: Professional Color Design**
*Screenshot: `Gradlette.png`, `Gradlette_Color_whele.png`, `Gradlette_Contrast Checker.png`, `Google_Explore Palette.png`*
A complete color ecosystem for motion designers.
- **Palette Generator**: Instantly create harmonious color palettes with the spacebar.
- **Image Extractor**: Capture any frame in AE and extract a professional color palette from it.
- **Contrast Checker**: Ensure your designs are WCAG compliant for perfect readability.
- **Color Wheel**: Visual harmonic exploration including Triadic, Analogous, and Complementary modes.

### **TemGrid: Precision Layout System**
*Screenshot: `TemGrid_Baseline_Grid.png`, `TemGrid_Golden_Ratio.png`, `TemGrid_Isometric.png`, `Templates.png`*
Achieve perfect composition with 9 professional grid systems.
- **Golden Ratio & Rule of Thirds**: Classic guides for cinematic framing.
- **Isometric Grid**: Build perfect 3D-style 2D layouts with 30° diagonal guides.
- **Native Guides**: Creates actual AE guide layers that respond to your comp size.

### **MoZoom: Precision Navigation**
*Screenshot: `Mozoom.png`*
Tired of the standard zoom presets? MoZoom gives you a precision slider to zoom your viewer from 1% to 800% instantly. It's touch-optimized and features a quick "Fit to Screen" button.

## 🛠️ Workflow & Optimization

### **MoHealth: Project Performance Analytics**
*Screenshot: `Mohealth.png`*
Keep your projects lean and fast. MoHealth analyzes your project organization and provides a "Health Score."
- **Cache Cleaning**: One-click purge of RAM, Disk Cache, and Database.
- **Render Hog Detection**: Identifies heavy effects slowing down your project.
- **Unused Asset Scanner**: Finds and removes project bloat in seconds.

### **MoF&R: Global Find & Replace**
*Screenshot: `MoF&R.png`*
The fastest way to edit text across multiple compositions. Search your entire project for specific strings and replace them globally with undo support.

### **MoTouch & MoHub**
*Screenshot: `Motouch.png`, `Mohub.png`*
- **MoTouch**: A customizable shortcut hub optimized for touch screens and tablets.
- **MoHub**: A centralized dashboard to launch and manage all MoChat tools.
