# MoChat Plugin: Complete Feature Breakdown

Detailed explanations for each of the 34 core features and interfaces in the MoChat ecosystem.

---

### 1. End-To-End Encryption (`End_to_end_encryption.png`)
MoChat prioritizes project security with industry-standard E2EE. This ensures that your sensitive client discussions, project files, and media links remain private and can only be read by the intended recipients. Stay HIPAA and GDPR compliant while collaborating on high-value motion design projects.

### 2. Google Explore Palette (`Google_Explore Palette.png`)
Never run out of inspiration. This feature integrates a vast library of trending color palettes from the Google Material Design ecosystem. Browse, filter, and instantly apply professional color schemes to your After Effects compositions with a single click.

### 3. Google Fonts Integration (`Google_Font.png`)
Stop wasting time searching for fonts in a browser. MoChat brings the entire Google Fonts library directly into After Effects. Preview thousands of typefaces with custom text, download them instantly, and apply them to your text layers without ever leaving your workspace.

### 4. Smart Palette Generator (`Google_Generate.png`)
Harness the power of the Google palette engine to generate harmonic color schemes on the fly. Whether you need a monochromatic look or a vibrant triadic scheme, this tool provides logical color transitions that elevate the professional quality of your designs.

### 5. Google Icons Library (`Google_Icons.png`)
Access thousands of high-quality vector icons from the Material Symbols library. Search by keyword and drag-and-drop icons directly into your project. Perfect for UI/UX animation, dashboard design, and infographics.

### 6. AI Image Color Extractor (`Google_Image Extractor.png`)
Match your graphics to your source footage perfectly. Upload an image or capture a frame from your active composition, and MoChat's AI will extract a professional 5-color palette based on the dominant and accent tones in the image.

### 7. Global Design Library (`Google_My Librarry.png`)
A centralized place to organize all your favorite design assets. Save specific fonts, color palettes, and icons into your "My Library" to reuse them across different projects, ensuring consistent branding for recurring clients.

### 8. Gradlette Color Suite (`Gradlette.png`)
The "Gradlette" main interface is your hub for color management. It features a spacebar-driven random generator that follows color theory rules, allowing you to discover unique color combinations in seconds.

### 9. Harmonic Color Wheel (`Gradlette_Color_whele.png`)
A visual playground for color theory. Explore Analogous, Triadic, Complementary, and Tetradic relationships with a precision wheel. Adjust saturation and lightness in real-time to refine your project's mood.

### 10. WCAG Contrast Checker (`Gradlette_Contrast Checker.png`)
Professional design requires professional accessibility. This tool checks the contrast ratio between your background and foreground colors to ensure your text is readable for all users and meets international WCAG 2.1 accessibility standards.

### 11. Secure Group Chat (`Group_Chat.png`)
Bring the whole team together in one secure thread. Create dedicated chat rooms for specific projects, allowing producers, designers, and animators to share feedback, links, and assets in a centralized, encrypted environment.

### 12. Global Find & Replace (`MoF&R.png`)
Modify text across your entire project in seconds. Search through all compositions for specific words or phrases and replace them globally. It supports case sensitivity and provides a preview before applying changes.

### 13. MoPrompt: AI Director (`MoPrompt.png`)
The revolutionary AI prompt builder integrated with After Effects. It features a 3D scene preview where you can move cameras and subjects to visualize your shot before generating high-end AI images or animations (Midjourney/DALL-E) to use in your project.

### 14. MoChat Main Dashboard (`Moachat_home_page.png`)
The "Home" of the plugin. This centralized view displays your active conversations, recent notifications, and quick-launch icons for the entire suite of MoChat tools, keeping your workflow organized.

### 15. MoFix: Project Repair (`Mofix.png`)
A dedicated utility for repairing project-level issues. From relinking broken media paths to cleaning up messy project structures, MoFix automates the boring parts of project maintenance so you can focus on being creative.

### 16. MoHealth: Performance Analytics (`Mohealth.png`)
Is your project lagging? MoHealth identifies "Performance Killers" like heavy effects, unoptimized assets, and massive disk caches. It gives your project a health score and provides one-click optimizations to speed up your render times.

### 17. MoHub Ecosystem (`Mohub.png`)
MoHub is the bridge between your technical tools and creative assets. It provides a seamless transition between the chat collaboration tools and the high-end design utilities like Gradlette and TemGrid.

### 18. Moji: Contextual Emoji Search (`Moji.png`)
Modern communication and design often require emojis. Moji provides a fast, searchable interface for thousands of emojis, allowing you to insert them into your chat messages or directly into After Effects text layers.

### 19. MoPack: Video Review Player (`Mopack.png`)
The heart of MoChat's collaboration suite. A frame-accurate video player that allows you to draw annotations and leave timestamped comments. Teammates can reply to specific frames, and these comments can be synced back to your AE timeline as markers.

### 20. MoTouch Macro Deck (`Motouch.png`)
Transform any touch device into a professional shortcut hub. Create custom layouts of your most-used After Effects commands and trigger them with a single tap, significantly reducing your reliance on complex keyboard shortcuts.

### 21. MoZoom: Precision Navigation (`Mozoom.png`)
standard zoom presets can be limiting. MoZoom provides a smooth slider that allows you to zoom your AE viewer from 1% to 800% with precision, making it easier to work on fine details or view the entire canvas.

### 22. Pixabay Integrated Stock (`Pixabay.png`)
Search millions of free, high-resolution photos, videos, and music tracks without leaving the plugin. Drag any asset directly into your project to use as a placeholder, texture, or final element.

### 23. Shared Actions Hub (`Share_Action.png`)
A dedicated menu for sharing project data. Whether you need to export a specific frame for review, share a color palette with a teammate, or send a project-sync request, all sharing actions are consolidated here for speed.

### 24. Smart Attachment System (`Smart_Attach.png`)
Collaboration often involves sharing files. This system intelligently recognizes After Effects project files, presets, and common media formats, ensuring they are shared securely and can be downloaded/opened directly by other team members.

### 25. TemGrid: Baseline Grid (`TemGrid_Baseline_Grid.png`)
Achieve perfect vertical rhythm in your typography. This grid guide provides consistent horizontal lines to ensure all your text layers are aligned perfectly, essential for professional UI and editorial animations.

### 26. TemGrid: Center Cross (`TemGrid_Center_Cross.png`)
The simplest yet most essential guide. It overlays a precise crosshair at the mathematical center of your composition, making it easy to center subjects or align logos perfectly.

### 27. TemGrid: Column Grid (`TemGrid_Column_Grid.png`)
Standard 12-column (or custom) layout guides. Use these to structure complex informational layouts, dashboards, and web-stye designs with consistent margins and gutters.

### 28. TemGrid: Golden Ratio (`TemGrid_Golden_Ratio.png`)
Apply the "Phi" ratio (1.618) to your compositions. This guide helps you place elements in a way that is naturally pleasing to the human eye, used by master painters and filmmakers for centuries.

### 29. TemGrid: Isometric Grid (`TemGrid_Isometric.png`)
Creating 2.5D graphics has never been easier. This tool generates 30° degree diagonal guides, allowing you to build perfect isometric shapes and layouts without needing complex 3D software.

### 30. TemGrid: Modular Grid (`TemGrid_Modular_Grid.png`)
Beyond simple columns, the modular grid creates a matrix of "zones." This is perfect for complex screen designs where you need to balance text, data, and imagery in a tight, organized space.

### 31. TemGrid: Pixel Grid (`TemGrid_Pixel_Grid.png`)
When every pixel counts. This fine mesh guide helps you align your shape paths and text to the pixel grid, preventing "blurry" edges and ensuring your graphics look crisp on all screens.

### 32. TemGrid: Rule of Thirds (`TemGrid_Rule_of_Thirds.png`)
The standard for photographic and cinematic composition. This 3x3 grid helps you place points of interest on the intersections for a more dynamic and balanced frame than simple centering.

### 33. TemGrid: Safe Areas (`TemGrid_Safe_Areas.png`)
Ensure your content works on every screen. Toggle between Action Safe and Title Safe guides to make sure your important information isn't cut off by different TV aspect ratios or social media UI overlays.

### 34. Professional Design Templates (`Templates.png`)
Don't start from scratch. Browse a library of pre-built composition templates with professional layouts already established. Load them into your project to save hours of setup time.
