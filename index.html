MoPack: Video Review Player

The heart of MoChat's collaboration suite. A frame-accurate video player that allows you to draw annotations and leave timestamped comments. Teammates can reply to specific frames, and these comments can be synced back to your AE timeline as markers.

Highlight

MoFix: Project Repair

A dedicated utility for repairing project-level issues. From relinking broken media paths to cleaning up messy project structures, MoFix automates the boring parts of project maintenance so you can focus on being creative.

Google Explore Palette

Never run out of inspiration. This feature integrates a vast library of trending color palettes from the Google Material Design ecosystem. Browse, filter, and instantly apply professional color schemes to your After Effects compositions with a single click.

Global Find & Replace

Modify text across your entire project in seconds. Search through all compositions for specific words or phrases and replace them globally. It supports case sensitivity and provides a preview before applying changes.

Google Fonts Integration

Stop wasting time searching for fonts in a browser. MoChat brings the entire Google Fonts library directly into After Effects. Preview thousands of typefaces with custom text, download them instantly, and apply them to your text layers without ever leaving your workspace.

Google Icons Library

Access thousands of high-quality vector icons from the Material Symbols library. Search by keyword and drag-and-drop icons directly into your project. Perfect for UI/UX animation, dashboard design, and infographics.

Features Collection

TemGrid Composition Guides

Professional layout grids overlaying your viewer ensure your designs have perfect rhythm and balance.

TemGrid: Rule of Thirds

The standard for photographic and cinematic composition. This 3x3 grid helps you place points of interest on the intersections for a more dynamic and balanced frame than simple centering.

MoZoom: Precision Navigation

standard zoom presets can be limiting. MoZoom provides a smooth slider that allows you to zoom your AE viewer from 1% to 800% with precision, making it easier to work on fine details or view the entire canvas.

Moji: Contextual Emoji Search

Modern communication and design often require emojis. Moji provides a fast, searchable interface for thousands of emojis, allowing you to insert them into your chat messages or directly into After Effects text layers.

MoHealth: Performance Analytics

Is your project lagging? MoHealth identifies "Performance Killers" like heavy effects, unoptimized assets, and massive disk caches. It gives your project a health score and provides one-click optimizations to speed up your render times.

MoTouch Macro Deck

Transform any touch device into a professional shortcut hub. Create custom layouts of your most-used After Effects commands and trigger them with a single tap, significantly reducing your reliance on complex keyboard shortcuts.
