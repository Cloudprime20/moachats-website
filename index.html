What's Inside

Everything Your Team Needs

Built specifically for motion designers and video editors who live in After Effects.

Encrypted Team Chat

Send messages, share DMs, and collaborate securely. E2EE ensures your sensitive client files and projects remain completely private.

MoPack Cloud Review

Instantly upload renders and share a web link. Clients can drop timestamped feedback and shape annotations right in their browser.

MoHealth Optimization

Scan your project for memory-hogging assets, duplicate footage, and instantly clear After Effects caches to speed up your workflow.

MoPrompt AI Expressions

Don't know code? Just type what you want to achieve, and MoPrompt's advanced AI will instantly generate and apply the ExtendScript or expression.

MoTouch Pie Menus

Speed up repetitive actions. Trigger specialized scripts, macros, and alignments using high-speed, intuitive radial menus.

Integrated Free Assets

Search Pixabay images, Google Fonts, and SVG icons directly within the panel and apply them straight to your timeline.

TemGrid Layouts

Toggle Rule of Thirds, Golden Ratio, Isometric, and Safe Area overlays onto your composition instantly for perfect alignments.

MoZoom Navigation

No more struggling with dense keyframes. Glide through your timeline and navigate layers with absolute precision and ease.

Get Feedback Fast.

Upload a video to MoPack, click Share, and instantly get a link anyone can open in a browser — no login required to view, login to comment. Reviewers can drop timestamped notes and draw directly on the video.

  • Timestamped comments + shape annotations
  • Shareable link — works on any device
  • Mark feedback as solved / rejected
  • Unlimited videos, unlimited reviewers
